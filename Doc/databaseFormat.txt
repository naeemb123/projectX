Table column:

<<standard pricing>>

  <price>:<number of flavours limit>:<price increase if flavours chosen is beyond flavours limit>
  example: 12.00:3:4.00 --> upto 3 flavours can be chosen at the price of £12.00. if more flavours chosen then there's
                            an extra £4.00 charge

<<special_pricing>>

  <price>
  example: 16.00 --> £16 for this flavour (e.g. blue mist, fruit burst etc ..)


<<type in selection flavours>>

    either: 'standard' or 'special' is accepted

<<unavailable in cafe>>

    either: 0 or 1
    0 --> cafe is unavailable
    1 --> cafe is available
